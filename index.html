REVENUES AND GRADES FOR U.S. SCHOOLS BY STATE

Soumitro Choudhury

10/14/2018

CSE 578 : Data Visualization

The first visualization is a chloropleth map of the United States providing the overview. The area marks on the map are filled with colors depending on the total revenue for grade schools. This map visualizes the total revenue for grade schools by state in the year 2015. Hovering over a state displays the total revenue for the state and also highlights the dot corresponding to that state on the scatter plot.

The second visualization is a scatter plot providing the details view. It shows the the average 8th grade scores in Math for each state in the year 2015. Hovering over a dot displays the name of the state and the average score.

In conclusion, these visualizations make clear that a larger revenue does not necessarily result in better quality of education and higher scores.
